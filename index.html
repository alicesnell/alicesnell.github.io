Control the spice in any food dish

The Spiceometer is able to measure the amount of spice in any dish. Spice is subjective, what is spicy for person could be quite mild for the next. With the spiceometer you can choose the exact spice level you like. The spiceometer also makes to possible to have the same amount of spice in a dish everytime. With a sleek stainless steal design the Spiceometer is durable and easy to wash.

The Scoville Scale Spice Measurement

The scoville scale is a measurement of heat in chilli peppers. The measurement starts at 0 units meaning no spice at all (bell peppers) and goes up to 2,480,000 units meaning extreme spice (carolina reaper). Using the scoville scale the spiceometer is able to detected the exact amount of spice in food. The measurements on the Spiceometer are easy to read, highlighting the units with the most distinct changes in spice level.

For personal use or commerical use

There is a dial on the side of the Spiceometer which allows you to mark the amount of spice you like. It is also great for restaurants. Instead of having to ask for mild, medium, or spicy, not knowing what to expect, now each customer can choose the amount of scoville units they would like. Making it easy for both the kitchen and the customer. With the spiceometer you get the spice you want every single time!
